How to Apply a Different Theme

To change your background theme:

  1. Click the "Apply This Theme" button above for your preferred design
  2. Or manually add the theme class to your hero section in index.html
  3. For example, to use the tech theme: <section id="home" class="s-home page-hero target-section tech-theme">

Available theme classes:

  • tech-theme - Dark professional with geometric patterns
  • warm-theme - Warm pink/coral gradients
  • cool-theme - Cool mint/pastel gradients
  • dark-theme - Modern dark with purple accents
  • No class - Default colorful animated gradient
